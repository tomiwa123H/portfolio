Aurelia Tailored Trousers

The trouser that holds everything else.

We spent eleven months on this trouser. Not because it's technically complicated — but because we wanted it to be right in a specific way that's hard to describe until you're wearing it: the way it makes you feel like you didn't try.

Italian wool-blend. Wide-leg, mid-rise. Structured waistband with a hidden hook that doesn't dig. TENCEL™ lining, cool and imperceptible. The seams are flat-felled at every point that matters. When you put it on, the first thought is: yes.

The Making

Eleven months.
One trouser.

Four fabric samples. Three waistband constructions. Two lining weights. The first version was good. The second was better. The third was the one every person who wore it kept reaching for.

It left Porto in January. Each piece in this batch was checked by hand before it shipped. 312 units made. Not 313.
