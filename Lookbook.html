"Every piece in this collection was designed around a single constraint: would I wear it in ten years?"
Director's note Shot over two days in an unrestored Pombaline building in Alfama, Lisbon. No artificial lighting. No retouching beyond dust removal. The clothes were the point — we wanted nothing competing with them.

Each look was assembled the morning of the shoot. No stylist. The pieces had to work without intervention. They did.
