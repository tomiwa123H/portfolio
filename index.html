FAQ

Common questions.

Straight answers — no fluff.

How long does it take to build my site?
Landing pages take 5–7 days. Full business websites take 10–14 days. Larger systems with dashboards take up to 21 days. I'll give you an exact timeline before we start.
Do I need to be in the UK to work with you?
No. I work fully remotely with clients in the UK, Nigeria, and internationally. All communication is via email or WhatsApp, and I work to UK business hours where needed.
What do you need from me to get started?
Your business name, what you do, a brief on your customers, any existing branding (logo, colours), and examples of sites you like. I'll handle everything else.
Will I be able to update the site myself?
Yes. I can build on WordPress for easy self-editing, or I'll write clean HTML that any developer can update. I also offer a 30-day support window on Premium packages.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. I typically take 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For larger projects, we can discuss a milestone-based payment schedule.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
Every package includes revision rounds. I share work-in-progress before final delivery so you're never surprised. Your satisfaction before final payment is guaranteed.
