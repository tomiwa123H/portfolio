Orders & Shipping
How long will my order take? +
Standard UK delivery takes 3–5 business days (£4.95, free on orders over £150). Express delivery is 1–2 business days (£9.95). International orders ship via DHL — typically 5–10 days for Europe, 7–14 days elsewhere. We ship Monday to Friday. Orders placed before 1pm on a business day usually leave the same day.
Can I change or cancel my order? +
We can make changes or cancellations within 2 hours of placing your order. Email care@aurelia.co with your order number immediately. After this window, the order will have left us — please return it when it arrives and we'll process a full refund.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes — we ship to over 40 countries via DHL Express, fully tracked. Import duties and taxes may apply on arrival and are the responsibility of the recipient. We mark all international shipments at full declared value; we won't mark items as gifts or undervalue shipments.
What if my order arrives damaged? +
Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage and your order number. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund immediately — no argument, no process. Damaged items don't need to be returned.
Returns & Refunds
What's your returns policy? +
Free returns within 30 days of receipt. Items must be unworn and unwashed, with all tags attached and in original packaging. Email care@aurelia.co with your order number — we'll send a prepaid returns label within 24 hours. We don't charge a restocking fee and we don't make returns difficult.
How long do refunds take? +
We inspect returned items within 2 business days of receiving them and process refunds the same day. The money typically appears in your account within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card provider. We'll email you when the refund is processed.
Can I exchange a size? +
We don't do direct exchanges because we can't guarantee stock when your return arrives. The fastest route is: return the item for a refund, then place a new order in the correct size. If the item you need is low in stock, email us — we'll hold a unit for 48 hours while your return is in transit.
Sizing & Fit
Does AURELIA run true to size? +
Broadly yes, with variations by silhouette. Tailored pieces run true with ease built in — we don't cut tight. Knitwear is intentionally generous; size down for a closer fit. Each product page includes specific fit notes, and our model's measurements are listed with every item.
I'm between sizes. What do I do? +
It depends on the piece. For tailored items like trousers and jackets, go down for a cleaner silhouette, up for comfort and drape. For knitwear, go down — the pieces are cut generous. You're always welcome to email us with your measurements and we'll advise directly.
Do you offer alterations? +
We don't offer alterations on new pieces, but our repair programme (for existing AURELIA customers) can sometimes accommodate minor adjustments. Email care@aurelia.co with details and photos — we'll let you know what's possible.
Materials & Care
What certifications do your materials hold? +
All cotton is GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) certified. Our wool is sourced from mills with current RWS (Responsible Wool Standard) certification. TENCEL™ lyocell carries the EU Ecolabel. We publish full material specifications on each product page — we don't use vague terms like "natural" or "eco" without a certification to back it.
How should I care for my pieces? +
Each piece has care instructions on its label and product page. As a general rule: hand wash cold or dry clean for wool and tailored pieces; machine wash cold on a delicate cycle for cotton and jersey. Never tumble dry knits or tailored garments — lay flat to dry. Well-cared wool genuinely improves with wear.
My piece has a small fault. What do I do? +
Email care@aurelia.co with photos and your order number. Manufacturing faults are repaired free of charge, regardless of when you bought the piece. We stand behind everything we've ever made.
Do you have a repair programme? +
Yes — we repair every piece we've ever made. Manufacturing defects are repaired free. General wear-and-tear repairs (loose seams, replaced buttons, relined pockets) are charged at cost — typically £15–£40 depending on the work. Email us with photos and we'll give you a quote within 48 hours. Ownership shouldn't end when a seam gives.
